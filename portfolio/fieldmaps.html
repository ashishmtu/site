Pavement Inspection Dashboard

ArcGIS Dashboard

• The traffic department's payment rating app visualizes data on an interactive map, allowing inspectors to visualize live inspection data collection and compare current ratings with previous years and sort by pavement type.
• Users can click on segments for more details and select specific segments to get the details.
• The app simplifies the process of identifying and working on poorly rated road segments, eliminating the need for printed sheets and manual identification as so far the department was working on collecting data mannually on printed sheets and then re-entering the collected data on excel sheets. This app saved the department months of inspection time and money.
